Hi There, My name is Adam Clemons,

Assistant Professor at SSDS!

I’m Adam Clemons, an Assistant Professor of Scholar Support and Data Services, with a keen focus on Digital Humanities. I love collaborating with diverse teams to promote and support the use of digital scholarship tools and methodologies across various fields. Right now, I’m working on an exciting project called “Dear Mr. Meredith,” where I’m mapping letters sent to James Meredith during his pursuit of admission to the University of Mississippi, categorizing them by sentiment. It’s a fascinating way to explore historical perspectives and their emotional undercurrents!
